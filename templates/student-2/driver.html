Drivr is a web app created intended to help tourists and locals alike plan a road trip around our beautiful country of New Zealand. All you have to do is enter the length of your trip, choose a vehicle, plan your route, and Drivr will output all of your estimated costs from fuel and car rental as well as give you directions on a map interface.

Drivr was built with pure JavaScript with Leaflet Maps, and includes some of the following features:

  • Automatic route calculation and full route customisation through Leaflet Routing Machine.
  • Dynamic calculations of all costs and statistics. Change your route, vehicle, or any other aspect of your trip and all calculations and costs are dynamically updated.
  • JSON data stores everything you need to know about 12 vehicles. Seats, engine size, fuel economy, doors, boot size, and rental cost.
  • Extensibility to import API data for car rental.
  • UX and usability focussed.
